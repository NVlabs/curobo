Volumetric Mapping
==================

.. automodule:: curobo.examples.getting_started.volumetric_mapping
   :no-members:

Once you have run the tutorial, open
:mod:`curobo.examples.getting_started.volumetric_mapping` in your editor.
The inline comments walk through the key design decisions: why depth is
filtered before integration, how the static geometry channel differs from
depth-fused surfaces, and why the ESDF is generated at a coarser resolution
than the TSDF.
